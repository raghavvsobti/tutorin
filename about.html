About Us

Lighting the spark within you

WHO WE ARE!

At Tutor IN, our mission is to make quality online learning affordable and accessible to anyone with an internet connection.Tutor IN is an extension of what education was originally intended to be; a meaningful one-on-one relationship with a mentor who would impart knowledge, offer guidance and ignite a life-long love of learning. Tutor IN is comprised of educators and administrators from many different walks of life. We know how hard teachers work to individualize and differentiate instruction in order to make a personalized connection with each student. We also know the inherent challenges each teacher faces in trying to accomplish this daunting task. This is where Tutor IN can help.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make quality online learning affordable and accessible to anyone with an internet connection. We are dedicated to helping students learn and understand the key concepts of a continuously changing curriculum.

Our Vision

Our vision is to help students of all ages succeed and achieve goals on behalf of the quality education we provide. Tutor IN started with a vision, a vision that is still apparent during each online tutoring session we deliver. We use only the highest quality, trained, seasoned, and educated professionals. We don't need to speak about it, our services speak for themselves.

Our Commitment

Our commitment to you is a 100 % satisfaction guarantee. If you are not completely satisfied with your online tutoring experience, we request you to contact us and our team will take the necessary steps to solve any problems encountered with our sessions, or your money back.
